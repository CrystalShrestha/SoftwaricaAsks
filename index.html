Features Services

Coding Help

All your Coding answers and solutions

Our Codementor/> feature helps you to get the best of your coding situation. We provide you the best of the best coding solutions. Building a strong Coding Community is how we will help you to get all the solutions to your coding problems.

Module questions

Course solution and help

Softwarica College's modules are designed to help you to get the best of your learning situation. Any help needed are provided by you to you. Creating a strong community for module related questions and answers is going to be very helpful for your learning.

Ask a Question

Ask your Coding or Module related queries

You can ask your own question to the community. The best answers will be given with votes and comments. Strong community with smart students will be participating in the forum for getting and giving help.

Jobs and Career

Career guidance and in-college Jobs

Guidance will be provided to you for your career. We will also provide you jobs in the college for polishing your skills. Personal Career guidance appointment can also be managed if necessary. In-College jobs will be provided to the interested ones.

What is Softwarica

Softwarica College of IT and E-Commerce

Softwarica College is working in collaboration with Coventry University to offer a range of undergraduate programmes. On successful completion students will receive Coventry University degree award. At Softwarica, we promote the idea of learning, informing and empowering for a brighter tomorrow. In recent times, when our students can educate themselves on multiple aspects at the same time on the internet, we would like to provide them the platform to be the best that they can be in the tech world.
